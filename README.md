# TARAX-WG
## Vision
Tarax is an experiment with blockchain-based AI governance for News and Social Media.
A system where AI generates news and users decide what inputs the system uses and what parameters it has through state of art governance mechanisms.

## Mission
To explore the potential benefits, challenges, and feasibility of using decentralized governance in conjunction with artificial intelligence to improve transparency, accuracy, and trustworthiness within the news and social media ecosystem.
